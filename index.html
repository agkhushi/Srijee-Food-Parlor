About Us

Our Story & Tradition

Welcome to Srijee Food Parlor, where tradition meets innovation. Our journey began with a simple mission: to serve authentic Indian cuisine with a modern twist. We take pride in our rich culinary heritage and strive to bring the authentic flavors of India to your plate.

Our chefs combine traditional recipes with contemporary cooking techniques to create dishes that are both authentic and innovative. We use only the finest ingredients, carefully selected to ensure the highest quality in every dish we serve.
